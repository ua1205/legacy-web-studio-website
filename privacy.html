1. Who We Are

Legacy Web Studio is a web design and digital marketing agency based in the United Kingdom, operating at legacywebstudio.co.uk. We specialise exclusively in web design and SEO services for estate planning and probate law firms.

For the purposes of UK GDPR, Legacy Web Studio is the data controller for personal data collected through this website. You can contact us at:

Website: legacywebstudio.co.uk

2. What Data We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal data:

2.1 Data you provide directly

When you complete our contact form or book a strategy call via Calendly, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Telephone number (if provided)
  • Firm or business name
  • The content of your message or enquiry

2.2 Data collected automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or search term
  • Date and time of your visit

This data is collected through cookies and analytics tools (see Section 8).

2.3 Data we do not collect

We do not collect special category data (such as health, ethnicity, or religious beliefs), payment card information, or data relating to children under 16.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

Purpose Data used
Responding to your enquiry or contact form submission Name, email, phone, message content
Scheduling a strategy call via Calendly Name, email, phone (processed by Calendly)
Sending service-related communications Name, email address
Improving our website and user experience Automatically collected analytics data
Complying with legal obligations Any data required by applicable law

We will never sell your personal data to third parties, or use it for purposes incompatible with those stated above without obtaining your prior consent.

4. Lawful Basis for Processing

We rely on the following lawful bases under UK GDPR Article 6:

  • Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f)): To respond to enquiries from potential and existing clients, and to improve our website and services. We have conducted a legitimate interests assessment and concluded that our interests are not overridden by your rights.
  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a)): Where you have expressly opted in to receive marketing communications from us. You may withdraw this consent at any time by contacting us at the address above.
  • Contractual necessity (Article 6(1)(b)): Where processing is necessary to perform a contract with you (for example, to deliver website design or SEO services).
  • Legal obligation (Article 6(1)(c)): Where we are required by law to process your data (for example, financial records for HMRC).

5. Third Parties

We may share your data with the following third-party service providers strictly for the purposes described:

5.1 Google Workspace

We use Google Workspace (Gmail, Google Docs) to manage business communications. Emails sent to usman@legacywebstudio.co.uk are processed by Google LLC. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

5.2 Calendly

When you book a strategy call through our Calendly link, your booking information (name, email, phone) is processed by Calendly LLC. Calendly's privacy policy is available at calendly.com/privacy.

5.3 GitHub Pages

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages (GitHub Inc., a subsidiary of Microsoft Corporation). GitHub may collect access logs including IP addresses as part of standard hosting operations. GitHub's privacy statement is available at docs.github.com.

5.4 Cloudflare

Our domain name is managed through Cloudflare, Inc., which may process some visitor data for the purposes of DNS resolution and DDoS protection. Cloudflare's privacy policy is available at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

All third-party processors are contractually required to process data only on our instructions and in accordance with applicable data protection law. No data is transferred outside the UK/EEA without appropriate safeguards in place.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes set out in this policy:

  • Enquiry and contact data: Retained for up to 2 years from the date of last contact, or until you request deletion.
  • Client project data: Retained for up to 6 years after the end of the business relationship, in accordance with standard UK business record-keeping requirements.
  • Website analytics data: Aggregated and anonymised data retained indefinitely; individually identifiable data deleted after 26 months.

When personal data is no longer required, it is securely deleted or anonymised.

7. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

You have the following rights in relation to your personal data:

  • Right of access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you (Subject Access Request).
  • Right to rectification: You may ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure: You may ask us to delete your data in certain circumstances ("the right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restriction of processing: You may ask us to limit how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability: You may ask us to provide your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: You may object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing purposes.
  • Rights related to automated decision-making: We do not use automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at usman@legacywebstudio.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days. There is no charge for exercising your rights unless requests are manifestly unfounded or excessive.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk if you believe we have not handled your data lawfully.

8. Cookie Policy

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website. This website currently uses the following categories of cookies:

8.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function and cannot be disabled. They do not store any personally identifiable information.

8.2 Analytics Cookies

We may use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our website. Analytics cookies collect anonymous information about page views, session duration, and traffic sources. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

8.3 Third-Party Cookies

Third-party services embedded in our site (such as Calendly) may set their own cookies. These are governed by the respective third party's privacy and cookie policies.

Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of the website. For guidance, visit allaboutcookies.org.
